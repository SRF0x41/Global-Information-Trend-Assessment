---
name: Prompt design philosophy
description: Prompts should give the model freedom to interpret, not just observe
type: feedback
---

Prompts in this project are designed to give the LLM interpretive freedom — to draw conclusions, make connections, and answer "so what?" — rather than constraining it to observation or summarization.

**Why:** The system's value is in synthesis, not data collection. The REFACTOR_PROMPT.md and EXTRACT_PROMPT.md both emphasize boldness, narrative voice, and grounded interpretation.

**How to apply:** When modifying or creating prompts, favor open-ended interpretive instructions over mechanical checklists. Give the model latitude to connect dots.
