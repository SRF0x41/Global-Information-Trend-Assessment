ITA is an autonomous agentic research framework that models the evolving zeitgeist — Western scope. It builds a provisional, revisable model of collective human experience across psychological, social, and cultural domains.

**Why:** Scoped to the West per user direction. The system is not a news aggregator; it's a cultural intelligence engine.

**Active flow:** search → extract → update → refactor. The refactor step produces `zeitgeist_report.md` — a polished cultural essay from the Living Document. Compare/Assess steps planned but not yet wired.

**How to apply:** When suggesting changes or running the system, respect the Western scope focus. Understand that the active flow now includes the Refactor step, not just search/extract.
